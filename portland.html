About Portland

Portland, Oregon, is the largest city in the state and is situated in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. Known for its vibrant arts scene, eco-friendly urban planning, and beautiful bridges, it lies along the banks of the Willamette River near its confluence with the Columbia River.

The city is renowned for its parks, bicycle paths, and dedication to sustainability. Additionally, Portland has a diverse culinary scene, marked by its pioneering food truck culture and a plethora of craft breweries.

Fun fact:
Portland was almost called Boston, Oregon, but the name was decided by a coin toss
