About Salem

Salem is the capital city of Oregon, nestled in the heart of the scenic Willamette Valley. Established in the early 1800s, it officially became the state's capital in 1851.

Today, besides being the hub of state government, Salem boasts a blend of historic landmarks, beautiful parks, and cultural institutions. With its vibrant community and diverse attractions, the city offers both residents and visitors a unique Pacific Northwest experience.

Fun fact:
Salem is nicknamed the "Cherry City", because of the past importance of the local cherry-growing industry.
